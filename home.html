WHY WE BUILT LINKWOK

Another late night, sickly sweet caffeine, underfed, overworked and in the habit of setting improbable deadlines (because well, what yardstick do we have to judge ourselves on but the collective tech genius of the ages!), but damn we love being a startup! And while that’s because we love hearing that we get to be our own bosses (blatant lie!), the honest truth is because we believe that between our world today, and a better one tomorrow is our own brand of secret sauce (it was important that I mention ‘secret sauce’).

And so, saddled with a couple of good ideas, we’ve bootstrapped- worked out of warehouses and accounting rooms in posh offices, ten year old IMacs and brand new Windows machines, basic IPhones and data dongles, trial versions of paid software and lots of food that comes in lots of little cardboard boxes. So basically whatever we could get our hands on. And that’s ok because we’re proud of what’s come out in the offing.
