EXPLORE

See how Linkwok works.
DROPZONE

Effortlessly curate from any website. Just click on the Linkwok icon on your browser and drop text, links, images and videos into the handy Linkbox that opens up on the bottom of your page! They'll be waiting for you in the Orphan Box tab on the Linkwok canvas.

NOTES AND TABLES

Add your own notes and tables to the Linkmap by dragging and dropping in exactly the same way as search results. They can be connected or teleported to, and individually styled and edited as well!

ORGANISE AND PREVIEW

Use the zoom feature to get a bird's eye view of the canvas, or focus on the smallest areas, all so that your map looks exactly as you intend it to! Click on each widget to open it as a preview whether it is search results, notes and tables or even groups!

COMMENT, LIKE, SHARE

Make your map public and go social! Share it with everybody, and see maps that others have made- drop comments and 'like' it to improve its visibility to others with similar questions.

SEARCH FOR PUBLIC MAPS

Public maps can be seen by everybody, commented on, liked and shared- you can search for public maps in exactly the same way as you would search for queries anywhere else on the internet!

MY MAPS

View all your maps in one convenient location, with descriptions, tags, comments and minimaps without needing to open it in another tab.

DESKTOP AND CLOUD CONNECTED

Add items to your Linkmap from your own computer or connected cloud storage (Evernote, Google Drive, Box and Skydrive) by, you've guessed it- drag and drop!
